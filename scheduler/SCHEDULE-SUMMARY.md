# Scheduler Summary

the key takeaway:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
  
  - Scheduler runs daily at 2:00 AM EST                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  - Monday is the peak day: 51 sites, ~90 articles (every site that publishes at all publishes on Monday)                                                                                                                                                        
  - Tuesday: 42 sites, ~83 articles
  - Wednesday: 44 sites, ~86 articles
  - Thursday: 44 sites, ~85 articles
  - Friday / Saturday / Sunday: 0 articles - nothing is scheduled

**Scheduler runs every day at 2:00 AM EST** (configured in `scheduler/config.yaml`).
It processes ALL sites on every run, but only generates articles for sites whose `preferred_days` include that day of the week.

**No articles are published on Friday, Saturday, or Sunday.**
