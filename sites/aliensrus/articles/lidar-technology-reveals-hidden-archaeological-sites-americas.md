## The Science Behind the Discoveries

Lidar technology operates by using laser pulses to penetrate vegetation and create detailed topographical maps of the ground beneath. According to reports, this technology can reveal ancient roads, mounds, and settlements that would otherwise remain invisible to traditional archaeological methods. The precision of these scans allows researchers to identify geometric patterns and artificial structures that suggest human construction and habitation.

The implications of these discoveries extend far beyond simple site identification. Caverns' team reportedly plans to conduct high-resolution drone scans to rebuild entire mound-builder cities in three-dimensional digital models, offering unprecedented insights into the sophisticated urban planning and construction techniques of ancient American civilizations.
