The Jerusalem Hills hide Ein Mata, described as a secret forest destination near the capital. This location offers a forested escape within a short drive of urban areas, providing a stark contrast to city life. The winter season enhances the forest experience with comfortable hiking conditions.

The southern destination features Harut Hill within the renowned Ramon Crater. Reports indicate this desert trail is particularly well-suited for the current season, when comfortable weather makes desert exploration more appealing. The location serves as an excellent introduction to crater geology for beginners.
