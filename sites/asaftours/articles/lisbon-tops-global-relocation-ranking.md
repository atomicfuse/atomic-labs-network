## Lisbon Claims Top Spot in Global Relocation Rankings

A comprehensive new ranking from Global Citizen Solutions has named Lisbon as the world's best city for relocation, evaluating destinations based on key factors including safety, healthcare, affordability, air quality, mobility, English proficiency, and social integration.

The Portuguese capital topped the list, followed by Amsterdam in second place and Melbourne rounding out the top three. The ranking reflects shifting priorities as remote work opportunities and digital nomad visas reshape where people choose to live globally.

## The Complete Top 10 Destinations

According to the Global Citizen Solutions ranking, the world's top relocation destinations are:

1. Lisbon
2. Amsterdam
3. Melbourne
4. Vienna
5. Barcelona
6. Singapore
7. Auckland
8. Tokyo
9. Copenhagen
10. Seoul

Tel Aviv, while placing 17th overall, also appeared on the index's list of most expensive cities, highlighting the complex tradeoffs potential relocators face when choosing their next home base.
