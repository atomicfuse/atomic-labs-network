### Key Features of a Top-Rated Dash Cam

When selecting a dash cam, several key features distinguish exceptional models from average ones. High-definition video recording is paramount, ensuring clear footage that can be used as evidence if needed. Additionally, a wide-angle lens is essential to capture as much of the road as possible. Other features like night vision, built-in GPS, and motion sensors add to the functionality of a dash cam, making them more versatile and reliable.
