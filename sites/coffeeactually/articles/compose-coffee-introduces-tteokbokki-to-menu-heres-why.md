## A New Twist in Café Culture

Compose Coffee's introduction of tteokbokki, a beloved Korean street food, marks a significant shift in how cafes can cater to local tastes. Known for its spicy and savory flavors, tteokbokki consists of chewy rice cakes often served in a spicy gochujang-based sauce. By integrating this dish into their menu, the café seeks to offer a unique culinary experience that differentiates it from typical coffee shops, which usually focus solely on beverages and pastries.

### Understanding the Korean Culinary Scene

Korean cuisine is renowned for its dynamic flavors and variety, and tteokbokki is no exception. Traditionally enjoyed as a street food snack, its popularity has surged in recent years, making it a staple in many casual dining establishments across Korea. By incorporating tteokbokki into its menu, Compose Coffee taps into this growing trend, appealing to both locals and tourists eager to explore authentic Korean flavors.
