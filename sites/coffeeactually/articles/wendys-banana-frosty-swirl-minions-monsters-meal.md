## New Frozen Treat Leads Movie-Themed Promotion

Wendy's is expanding its dessert offerings with the introduction of a new Banana Frosty Swirl, headlining the chain's latest limited-time Minions & Monsters meal promotion. The new frozen treat features banana cream sauce swirled into the restaurant's signature Vanilla Frosty base.

According to reports, the promotion is tied to Illumination's upcoming film and includes themed meal options for both children and adults, along with exclusive collectible toys designed to drive repeat visits during the summer movie season.

## Kids' Meal Options Already Available

The children's portion of the promotion launched earlier, offering young diners a choice between 2-piece chicken tenders, 4-piece chicken nuggets, hamburger, or cheeseburger. Each kids' meal comes with a drink and either junior fries or apple bites.

What sets this promotion apart is the collection of six exclusive toys featuring characters named James, Henry, Ed, Richard Goomi, and Dort. These collectibles are designed to encourage families to return multiple times to complete their collections.

## Adult Meals Launch June 15

Starting June 15, adult customers can participate in the promotion with specially designed meal combinations. The adult option includes a choice between the Big Bacon Classic or Spicy Chicken Sandwich, paired with small fries and a small serving of the new Banana Frosty Swirl.

Adults also have access to their own collectible items through four exclusive blind box Minions & Monsters collectibles, adding an element of surprise to each purchase.

## Expanded Beverage Menu

Beyond the signature Banana Frosty Swirl, Wendy's is introducing two new Coca-Cola Freestyle beverages as part of the promotion. The Pineapple Minion MischieFizz and Goomi's Glimey Lime offer themed drink options that complement the movie tie-in experience.

These specialized beverages are available exclusively through Wendy's Coca-Cola Freestyle machines, providing customers with unique flavors they can't find elsewhere.

## Strategic Timing for Summer Sales

The promotion comes at a crucial time for the fast-food chain, as movie tie-in promotions traditionally drive increased foot traffic during the summer blockbuster season. According to industry observations, collectible toys create powerful incentives for repeat visits, particularly when chains are focusing on value offerings to boost overall sales.

The limited-time nature of both the food items and collectibles is designed to create urgency among customers, encouraging them to visit multiple times before the promotion ends.

## Collectible Appeal Drives Engagement

The blind box format for adult collectibles adds an element of mystery that has proven successful in other fast-food promotions. With six different toys available in the kids' meals and four blind box options for adults, customers have multiple reasons to return throughout the promotion period.

This strategy taps into both nostalgia for movie-themed fast-food promotions and the fear of missing out on limited-edition merchandise that has become increasingly popular across various retail categories.

## Value Positioning in Competitive Market

The promotion represents Wendy's effort to compete in an increasingly crowded fast-food landscape where value offerings and unique experiences differentiate brands. By combining new menu items like the Banana Frosty Swirl with collectible elements, the chain aims to provide both immediate satisfaction and lasting appeal.

The dual approach of offering promotions for both children and adults acknowledges that family dining decisions often involve multiple stakeholders, each with their own preferences and interests.

Customers interested in the full Minions & Monsters experience can visit participating Wendy's locations now for kids' meals and collectibles, with adult meal options and the Banana Frosty Swirl becoming available June 15.
