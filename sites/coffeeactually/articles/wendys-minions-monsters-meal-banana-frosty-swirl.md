## Wendy's Teams Up with Illumination for Minions & Monsters Promotion

Wendy's has announced a new limited-time promotion tied to Illumination's upcoming Minions & Monsters film, featuring themed meals for both kids and adults along with exclusive collectible toys and a brand-new dessert option.

The promotion centers around specially curated meals that cater to different age groups, with the kids' portion already available and adult options launching June 15.

## Kids' Meal Options and Collectibles

According to reports, the kids' meal is currently available and offers several protein choices including 2-piece chicken tenders, 4-piece chicken nuggets, hamburger, or cheeseburger. Each meal comes with a drink and either junior fries or apple bites as the side option.

The kids' meal promotion features six exclusive collectible toys showcasing characters from the film, including James, Henry, Ed, Richard Goomi, and Dort. These collectibles are designed to create repeat-visit incentives for families looking to collect the complete set.

## Adult Meal Launches Mid-June

Starting June 15, Wendy's will introduce adult-focused meal options as part of the promotion. The adult meal includes a choice between the Big Bacon Classic or Spicy Chicken Sandwich, paired with small fries and a small serving of the new Banana Frosty Swirl.

For adult collectors, the promotion offers four exclusive blind box Minions & Monsters collectibles, adding an element of surprise to the dining experience.

## New Banana Frosty Swirl Takes Center Stage

The standout addition to Wendy's menu is the new Banana Frosty Swirl, a limited-time dessert that combines banana cream sauce swirled into the classic Vanilla Frosty base. This new flavor option represents Wendy's continued innovation in their signature Frosty lineup.

The Banana Frosty Swirl will be available as part of the adult meal package and likely as a standalone menu item during the promotional period.

## Specialty Beverages Join the Mix

Completing the themed menu offerings, Wendy's is introducing two new Coca-Cola Freestyle beverages exclusively for this promotion. The Pineapple Minion MischieFizz and Goomi's Glimey Lime provide additional tie-ins to the film's characters and themes.

These specialty drinks are expected to be available at participating locations with Coca-Cola Freestyle machines during the promotional window.

## Strategic Summer Blockbuster Timing

According to reports, the timing of this promotion aligns with the summer blockbuster season, when movie tie-in promotions typically drive increased foot traffic and social media engagement. The collectible aspect is particularly strategic, as it encourages multiple visits from customers seeking to complete their collections.

The promotion comes as Wendy's continues to focus on value offerings designed to boost sales, with the combination of entertainment tie-ins and exclusive merchandise serving as key differentiators in the competitive fast-food landscape.
