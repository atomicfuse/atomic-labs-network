As we step into 2026, the world of interior design is abuzz with exciting trends that promise to transform homes into stylish havens. Kristen McGowan, a renowned interior designer, shares her insights on the top 10 interior design and home decor trends for this year. Whether you're planning a full home makeover or looking to refresh your space, these trends offer inspiration for every room.

From bold color choices to innovative furniture designs, 2026 is all about embracing new ideas while keeping functionality in mind. Kristen highlights key trends across various spaces, including kitchens, bathrooms, and living areas. Discover what's in and what's out this year as we delve deeper into each trend.

## Kitchen Trends

Kitchens in 2026 are leaning towards minimalism, with clean lines and uncluttered spaces. The focus is on functionality with sleek appliances and smart storage solutions. Natural materials like wood and stone are making a comeback, adding warmth to the modern kitchen.

## Bathroom Trends

Bathrooms are becoming more spa-like, with trends favoring luxurious finishes and calming color palettes. Expect to see a lot of soft greens and blues, which create a serene atmosphere. Freestanding bathtubs and large walk-in showers are increasingly popular, offering a touch of indulgence.

## Color Trends

The color of the year for 2026 is a rich terracotta, bringing warmth and a touch of nature into interiors. This earthy tone pairs beautifully with neutrals and can be used across various elements, from walls to decorative pieces.

## Furniture Trends

Sustainability is key in furniture design, with more people opting for eco-friendly materials. Recycled and upcycled pieces are in vogue, reflecting a growing awareness of environmental impact. Modular furniture is also gaining popularity for its versatility and space-saving benefits.

## Decor Trends

In decor, personal expression is encouraged with unique art pieces and custom-made items. Textures play a significant role, with layered fabrics and mixed materials adding depth to spaces. Lighting is also becoming an important design element, with statement fixtures enhancing the overall aesthetic.

These trends offer a glimpse into the future of home interiors, where style meets practicality. Kristen McGowan's expert advice provides a roadmap for creating spaces that are both trendy and timeless. Whether you're updating a single room or redecorating your entire home, let these trends guide your design choices for 2026.
