Shopping for home decor can be both exciting and overwhelming, especially with the plethora of options available in 2026. Renowned interior designer Kristen McGowan guides us through the latest offerings at Target, highlighting the must-see pieces from popular collections such as The Spring Threshold by Studio McGee and Chip and Joanna Gaines' Hearth & Hand.

Kristen's keen eye for design helps navigate through Target's aisles, showcasing artificial plants, affordable furniture, and designer dupes that are perfect for sprucing up any living space. Whether you're looking to refresh your living room with stylish throw pillows or seeking household must-haves, this shopping trip is packed with inspiration.

## New Collections to Elevate Your Home

The Spring Threshold by Studio McGee Collection offers a range of sophisticated pieces that blend seamlessly into any interior design style. From chic furniture to elegant decor items, this collection provides the perfect balance of style and functionality.

Meanwhile, Chip and Joanna Gaines' Hearth & Hand Collection introduces a rustic charm to home decor. Known for their signature farmhouse style, this collection combines warmth with simplicity, bringing a cozy feel to any room.

## Spotlight on Affordable and Stylish Finds

One of the highlights of shopping at Target is the access to affordable furniture that doesn't compromise on style. Kristen points out several furniture pieces that serve as excellent designer dupes, allowing homeowners to achieve a high-end look without breaking the bank.

Artificial plants have also become a popular choice for those looking to add a touch of greenery to their homes without the maintenance. Target's selection includes lifelike options that can brighten up any space.

## Design Tips for a Cohesive Look

Kristen shares valuable interior design tips throughout the shopping experience. She emphasizes the importance of layering textures and incorporating different materials to create depth and interest in a room. Mix and match various decor elements to achieve a balanced and cohesive look.

For those looking to update their home decor in 2026, Kristen's Target shopping guide is a treasure trove of ideas and inspiration. With her expert advice and the latest collections, you can transform your living space into a stylish haven.

Discover more of Kristen McGowan's favorite Target finds and explore her design journey through her social media and blog. Happy shopping!
