## From Lighting to Lifestyle: A Strategic Expansion

In Common With, the cult-favorite lighting design studio founded by Nick Ozemba and Felicia Hung, is making waves in the design world with its latest venture beyond illumination. According to reports, the studio has launched the Lido collection, marking its first expansion into homewares through a strategic collaboration with Venice-based glassware company Laguna~B.

This move represents more than just product diversification—it signals the studio's evolution from a lighting-focused brand to what industry observers describe as a comprehensive lifestyle design ecosystem.

## Traditional Craftsmanship Meets Contemporary Design

The Lido collection showcases the intersection of heritage craftsmanship and modern design sensibilities. According to reports, the collaboration combines traditional Murano glassmaking techniques with contemporary design principles, featuring specialized methods including goti de fornasa and murrine techniques in the lighting designs.

This approach reflects a broader trend in the design industry where studios are scaling craft-based production while maintaining artisanal quality. The partnership with Laguna~B demonstrates how contemporary design brands can honor traditional techniques while making them accessible to modern consumers.
