## The Art of Making Budget Finds Look Luxe
Before we dive into the items, let’s explore some tricks interior designers use to make affordable furniture look more upscale. 

### Materials Matter
Materials like bouclé, natural wood, and metals can instantly elevate the perception of budget furniture. Textures add depth and interest, making pieces seem more luxurious.

### Play with Silhouettes 
Rounded shapes and clean lines can contribute to a more refined appearance. Designers often choose pieces like sofas or tables with these details for a sophisticated look.

### Choose the Right Finishes
Finishes like matte, brushed, or natural can transform a piece from basic to stunning. Look for items that employ unique finishes to enhance their visual appeal.
