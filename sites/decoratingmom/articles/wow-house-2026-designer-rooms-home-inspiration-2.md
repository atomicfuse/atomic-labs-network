## Global Designers Transform Chelsea Harbour Showcase

WOW!house has returned for its fifth year at Design Centre Chelsea Harbour, presenting an expanded showcase of 22 rooms and outdoor spaces that promise to inspire home decorators seeking fresh ideas for 2026. According to reports, the prestigious design showcase features work from both established and emerging designers from around the globe.

The 2026 edition offers remarkable diversity in design inspiration, spanning from traditional Georgian drawing rooms and Mediterranean courtyards to more contemporary interpretations including sculptural libraries and luxury bars. This range demonstrates how different design philosophies can be adapted for various home spaces and personal tastes.

## Featured Spaces and Designers

The showcase includes several standout installations that highlight different approaches to interior design. Garden Folly spaces feature contributions from Darren Price of Adam Architecture for the facade work, while Studio Enass has created the interior room design. The outdoor spaces are further enhanced by The Gardenists, who designed the Artorius Faber Entrance Garden.

Indoor spaces showcase equally impressive talent, with Francis Sultana designing the Entrance Hall and Albion Nord creating the Turnell & Gigon Group Drawing Room. For those interested in unique functional spaces, Roisin Lafferty has designed the Shepel' Library, while Elicyon presents the Lalique Home Bar.
