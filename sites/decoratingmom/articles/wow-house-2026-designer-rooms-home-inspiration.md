## Global Designers Transform Chelsea Harbour Showcase

WOW!house has returned for its fifth year at Design Centre Chelsea Harbour, presenting an impressive collection of 22 rooms and outdoor spaces that demonstrate the latest in interior design innovation. According to reports, this prestigious design showcase features work from both established and emerging designers from around the globe, offering homeowners a wealth of inspiration for their own spaces.

The 2026 edition spans an eclectic range of design styles, from Georgian drawing rooms and Mediterranean courtyards to sculptural libraries and luxury bars. This diverse approach provides visitors with ideas that can be adapted across different home styles and budgets.

## Featured Designer Spaces and Concepts

Several standout installations showcase the creative range on display this year. The Garden Folly Facade, created by Darren Price of Adam Architecture, demonstrates how architectural elements can transform outdoor spaces. Studio Enass has designed the corresponding Garden Folly Room, while The Gardenists present the Artorius Faber Entrance Garden.

Indoor spaces include Francis Sultana's Entrance Hall design and the Turnell & Gigon Group Drawing Room by Albion Nord. For those seeking unique focal points, Roisin Lafferty's Shepel' Library offers a sculptural approach to book storage and reading spaces. Elicyon rounds out the featured designs with the Lalique Home Bar, bringing luxury entertaining concepts to the showcase.
