## Global Designers Transform Chelsea Harbour Showcase

WOW!house has returned for its fifth year at Design Centre Chelsea Harbour, presenting an expanded showcase of 22 rooms and outdoor spaces created by both established and emerging designers from around the globe. According to reports, this year's edition offers inspiration ranging from Georgian drawing rooms and Mediterranean courtyards to sculptural libraries and luxury bars.

## Featured Spaces Span Multiple Design Styles

The 2026 showcase demonstrates remarkable diversity in design approaches and influences. Among the featured spaces are a Garden Folly Facade and Room created through collaboration between Darren Price of Adam Architecture and Studio Enass, and an Artorius Faber Entrance Garden designed by The Gardenists.

The interior spaces showcase equally impressive range, including an Entrance Hall by Francis Sultana, a Drawing Room by Albion Nord for the Turnell & Gigon Group, and a distinctive Library by Roisin Lafferty called "Shepel'." The showcase also features a Lalique Home Bar designed by Elicyon, described as resembling London's coolest speakeasy.
