## Designer Tricks to Make Cheap Furniture Look Expensive
So what makes these affordable pieces look chic and luxe? Here are some tips straight from interior designers:

### Play with Textures
Using materials like bouclé, wood grain, or velvet can instantly elevate the feel of your space. Look for furniture and decor that incorporate these rich textures, giving the illusion of high-end design.

### Focus on Silhouettes
Rounded edges, sleek lines, and unique shapes can make even the lowest-price items feel extravagant. Don't shy away from searching for pieces with interesting silhouettes!

### Add Luxe Finishes
Pay attention to details such as glass doors or gold accents. These elegant finishes are often found in high-priced pieces and can heighten the appearance of budget furniture.

### Mix and Match
Combining pricier items you already own with these Walmart finds can create an eclectic yet polished look. Don't hesitate to style budget accents alongside heirloom pieces or expensive decor.
