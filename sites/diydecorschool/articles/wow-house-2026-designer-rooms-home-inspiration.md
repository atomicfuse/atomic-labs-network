## Standout Spaces and Featured Designers

Among the notable installations, several spaces are drawing particular attention from visitors seeking home design inspiration. The showcase includes a Garden Folly Facade by Darren Price of Adam Architecture, paired with a Garden Folly Room designed by Studio Enass. The entrance experience begins with an Artorius Faber Entrance Garden created by The Gardenists.
