## Standout Spaces and Featured Designers

This year's WOW!house features several notable installations that highlight different aspects of contemporary design thinking. The Garden Folly Facade, created by Darren Price of Adam Architecture, demonstrates how classical architectural elements can be reimagined for modern spaces. Studio Enass has contributed the Garden Folly Room, while The Gardenists present the Artorius Faber Entrance Garden, showcasing how outdoor spaces can seamlessly integrate with interior design concepts.

Indoor spaces include Francis Sultana's Entrance Hall, which sets the tone for visitors entering the showcase. The Turnell & Gigon Group Drawing Room by Albion Nord offers insights into contemporary approaches to traditional living spaces, while Roisin Lafferty's Shepel' Library demonstrates how functional spaces can become sculptural statements. Elicyon rounds out the featured spaces with the Lalique Home Bar, presenting ideas for creating sophisticated entertaining areas.
