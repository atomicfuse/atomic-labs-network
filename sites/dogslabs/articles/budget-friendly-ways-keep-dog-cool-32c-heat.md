## Five Budget-Friendly Cooling Solutions

Pet care experts have identified five simple methods to help dogs and cats beat the heat without breaking the bank, using items most households already have available.

### 1. Bottle It

According to the guidance, frozen bottles provide an effective cooling solution for pets during heatwaves. This method offers a simple way to create a cooling station using standard household items.

### 2. Ice Time

Ice-based cooling techniques represent another budget-friendly approach to helping pets manage extreme temperatures. This method can be implemented immediately with items found in most freezers.

### 3. Pet Popsicles

Creating DIY frozen treats for dogs offers both cooling relief and entertainment during hot weather. These homemade popsicles provide a cost-effective alternative to commercial pet cooling products.

### 4. Cardboard Cooler

Reports indicate that cardboard can be repurposed to create cooling solutions for pets. This recycling approach offers pet owners a virtually free method to provide heat relief.

### 5. Walk Smart

Timing walks appropriately becomes crucial during heatwave conditions. Pet owners need to understand how to determine if pavements are too hot for their dogs' paws and adjust walking schedules accordingly.
