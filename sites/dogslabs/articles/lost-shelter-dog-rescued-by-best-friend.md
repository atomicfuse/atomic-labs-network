This rescue highlights the importance of socialization programs in animal shelters. When dogs are given opportunities to interact and form relationships, they not only become more adoptable but also develop trust and emotional stability that can be life-saving in situations like Drake's.

The friendship between Drake and Georgia demonstrates what shelter experts know: dogs benefit tremendously from social interaction with other dogs, especially during stressful transitions.
