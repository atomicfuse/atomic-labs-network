## Timberwolves Star Becomes Cat Care Advocate

Minnesota Timberwolves center Rudy Gobert has stepped off the basketball court and into the world of animal advocacy, partnering with PETA for a campaign promoting responsible cat care. The NBA star, known for his defensive prowess on the court, is now applying those protective instincts to feline welfare alongside his beloved cats, Ace and Link.

## From Court Defense to Cat Protection

According to reports, Gobert's PETA campaign positions him as a "defender" for animals, drawing parallels between his role as a protective player and responsible pet ownership. The campaign leverages basketball terminology to make cat care messaging both fun and memorable for audiences.

The PSA features Gobert sharing essential cat care basics that every responsible pet owner should know. These include providing appropriate toys for mental stimulation, maintaining clean litter boxes, ensuring indoor safety for cats, promoting adoption from shelters, and most importantly, never declawing cats.

## Essential Cat Care Guidelines

The campaign emphasizes several critical aspects of responsible cat ownership that align with PETA's animal welfare mission. Indoor cats live safer lives, protected from traffic, predators, and disease. The importance of spaying and neutering pets helps control overpopulation, while adoption from shelters gives homeless animals second chances.

Perhaps most significantly, the campaign addresses the controversial practice of declawing, which many veterinarians and animal welfare organizations oppose as unnecessary and harmful to cats' natural behavior and wellbeing.
