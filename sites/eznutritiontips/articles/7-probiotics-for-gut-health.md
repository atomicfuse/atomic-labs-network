## What Are Probiotics?

Probiotics are live microorganisms that provide health benefits when consumed in adequate amounts. They’re often known as “good” or “friendly” bacteria and can have significant impacts on gut flora, aiding in digestion and contributing to overall health. Therefore, selecting the right strains and formulations is vital for achieving your health goals.

## Why Choosing the Right Probiotic Matters

When selecting a probiotic, consider factors such as strain specificity, CFU count, and third-party testing. Not all probiotics are created equal! 

### Strain Specificity

Different strains serve different purposes—some help with immune support, while others may target gut health or even mental wellness. It’s vital to match the strain with your specific health goal.

### CFU Counts

While CFU (colony-forming units) count is important, it shouldn’t be the only factor you consider when choosing a supplement. An effective probiotic will have the right balance of both strains and CFU.

### Third-Party Testing

Ensure your probiotic supplements have been verified through third-party testing. This is a good indicator of quality and helps exclude red flags.
