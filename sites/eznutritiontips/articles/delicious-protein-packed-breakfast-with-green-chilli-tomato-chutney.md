Starting your day with a nutritious breakfast sets the tone for a productive day ahead. If you're looking for a quick, protein-rich option, this steamed breakfast recipe paired with a flavorful green chilli tomato chutney is perfect for you.

This easy breakfast idea is designed for those hectic mornings when you need something quick yet satisfying. The recipe uses simple ingredients that are packed with nutrients, ensuring you get a healthy start. The combination of paneer and suji forms the base of a delicious dish that can be prepared in just 10 minutes.

## Why Choose a Protein-Rich Breakfast?

Protein is an essential nutrient that helps in building and repairing tissues. It also plays a crucial role in keeping you full for longer, reducing the temptation to snack between meals. Including a protein-rich breakfast can help maintain energy levels throughout the day and support overall wellness.

## The Recipe: Quick and Nutritious

This recipe highlights the use of paneer, a popular ingredient in Indian cuisine, known for its high protein content. Combined with suji (semolina), it creates a delightful dish that is both filling and delicious. The preparation is hassle-free, requiring no kneading or rolling, making it ideal for anyone on a tight schedule.

## Accompanying Green Chilli Tomato Chutney

The green chilli tomato chutney adds a spicy and tangy flavor that complements the steamed breakfast perfectly. This chutney is made with fresh ingredients, enhancing the dish's taste while providing additional health benefits. It's a versatile accompaniment that can be paired with various other dishes as well.

## Perfect for Tiffin Boxes

This breakfast recipe isn't just limited to morning meals. It can be easily packed into tiffin boxes for a nutritious lunch option. The ease of preparation and transportability make it a favorite among those who need meals on the go.

With such a quick and easy recipe, enjoying a healthy breakfast has never been simpler. Try this protein-packed breakfast with green chilli tomato chutney and experience the difference it makes in your daily routine.
