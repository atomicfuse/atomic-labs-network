## Why Protein Distribution Matters After 50

The analysis reveals that women over 50 require more evenly distributed protein throughout the day to support muscle maintenance, bone health, and healthy aging. For women navigating menopause and beyond, strategic protein timing can make a significant difference in maintaining metabolic health and muscle mass.
