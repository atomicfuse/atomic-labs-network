Valentine's Day is a perfect time to focus on wellness and add happiness to your daily routine. This week, we explore some simple self-care practices and healthy recipes that can easily fit into your lifestyle. Whether you're looking for a delicious pistachio latte, hearty turkey meatballs, or a quick trip to CVS for a Valentine's surprise, these tips and recipes are sure to bring joy and well-being to your days.

Starting with something special, let's look at how you can enjoy a healthier pistachio latte. Using Dunkin cold brew concentrate and pistachio butter, this latte is not only delightful but also packed with nutrients. It's a great way to start your morning routine with a boost of energy and flavor.

## Simple Recipes for Healthier Living

Incorporating easy recipes into your meal plan can be incredibly rewarding. For instance, homemade turkey meatballs are a delicious way to add protein to your diet. Pair them with your favorite sides for a complete meal that satisfies both your taste buds and nutritional needs.

If you're a fan of snacks, the high-fiber energy bites are perfect for keeping you energized throughout the day. Made with dates, oats, and a mix of seeds, these bites are not only tasty but also a great source of fiber and nutrients. Simply blend the ingredients and roll them into balls for a convenient snack that you can enjoy anytime.

## Self-Care and Surprises

Self-care is essential for maintaining wellness, and it doesn't have to be complicated. A simple walk outside can do wonders for your mental and physical health. Taking time for a 'hot girl walk' as part of your routine can boost your mood and provide a refreshing break from daily stress.

Valentine's Day is an excellent opportunity to surprise your loved ones with thoughtful gifts. CVS offers a range of affordable options under $10 that make it easy to show appreciation without breaking the bank. ExtraCare members can even enjoy additional savings, making it a practical choice for spreading love this season.

Incorporating these wellness tips and recipes into your life can make a significant difference in how you feel each day. From nourishing meals to moments of self-care, every small step counts towards a healthier, happier you.
