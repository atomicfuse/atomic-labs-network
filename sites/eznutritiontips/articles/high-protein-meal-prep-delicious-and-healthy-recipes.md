Meal prepping is not only a fantastic way to save time during the week, but it also ensures you are eating nutritious and delicious meals. In this guide, we will explore some high-protein and healthy recipes that are perfect for meal prep. Each recipe is designed to provide you with the nutrients you need to fuel your day.

First up is the **Breakfast Power Bowl**. This dish is a perfect way to start your day with a boost of energy. The combination of eggs, mushrooms, avocado, and pickled onions provides a delightful mix of flavors and textures. To prepare, begin by slicing mushrooms and avocado, and measuring out pickled onions. Sauté the mushrooms in a skillet with olive oil until tender, then set aside. Scramble the eggs in the same skillet and place them at the bottom of a bowl. Top with the mushrooms, avocado, and pickled onions for a nutritious breakfast.

## Beef or Chicken Milanesas

Next, we have Beef or Chicken Milanesas, a perfect protein-packed meal for lunch or dinner. You can choose between lean beef or chicken breasts as your main protein. Begin by mixing eggs with minced garlic, parsley, salt, pepper, and oregano. Coat the meat in the egg mixture, then in breadcrumbs, allowing it to rest before baking. Bake the coated meat in a preheated oven until golden brown and crispy for a delicious and healthy entrée.

## Kousa Mahshi

Lastly, we explore Kousa Mahshi, a stuffed zucchini dish. This Middle Eastern recipe features zucchinis filled with a mixture of minced lamb or beef, rice, tomatoes, and herbs. After preparing the zucchini and stuffing mixture, stack them in a pot with tomato passata, lemon juice, and seasoning. Simmer until the zucchinis are tender, and serve with the flavorful sauce. This dish is not only healthy but also a feast for the senses.

These recipes are sure to add variety to your meal prep routine while providing high-protein options to keep you satisfied and energized. Whether you're looking for breakfast, lunch, or dinner ideas, these dishes have you covered.
