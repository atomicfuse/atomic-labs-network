According to reports, Bitcoin's technical analysis reveals a complex picture with 12 positive moving average signals supporting the bullish case. The cryptocurrency has established firm support in the $79,500 to $80,000 range, while facing resistance between $81,100 and $82,800.

The current setup displays what analysts describe as a compression phase, which typically precedes explosive directional moves in either direction. While oscillators remain neutral, the moving averages continue to favor bullish momentum, creating an interesting tension in the technical landscape.
