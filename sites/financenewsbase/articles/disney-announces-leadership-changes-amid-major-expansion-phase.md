## Disney Restructures Leadership During Historic Growth Period

The Walt Disney Company has announced significant leadership changes across its Disney Experiences division, positioning new executives to oversee what the company describes as one of its biggest investment cycles ever. According to reports, three key appointments will reshape the leadership structure of Disney's most profitable division.

Joe Schott will assume leadership of Walt Disney World, while Natacha Rafalski moves to head Disney Signature Experiences, and Christophe Murphy takes over operations at Disneyland Paris. All appointments are set to take effect by the end of July, according to company announcements.

The leadership reshuffle comes as Disney accelerates major park, cruise, and resort expansion projects globally. Disney Experiences is entering what industry observers characterize as one of its most ambitious growth phases, with new lands, cruise growth, and international park expansions underway simultaneously.
