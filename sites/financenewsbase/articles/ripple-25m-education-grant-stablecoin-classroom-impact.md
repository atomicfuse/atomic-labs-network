## Cryptocurrency Makes Real-World Impact in Education

Ripple's ambitious $25 million education initiative has delivered measurable results in its first year, demonstrating how cryptocurrency can address tangible social challenges. According to reports, most of the funding was distributed through Ripple's RLUSD stablecoin, making this one of the largest crypto-backed nonprofit grants ever recorded.

The funding allocation reveals a strategic approach to educational impact. DonorsChoose received $15 million, which enabled funding for more than 48,000 classroom projects across the United States. Meanwhile, Teach For America received $10 million to support 2,300 teachers, ultimately reaching over 270,000 students.

The use of RLUSD stablecoin for grant distribution represents a significant development in how cryptocurrency can serve as infrastructure for charitable giving.
