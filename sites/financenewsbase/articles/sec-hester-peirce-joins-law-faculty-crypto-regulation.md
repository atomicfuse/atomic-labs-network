Peirce, widely known in crypto circles as "Crypto Mom" for her generally supportive stance toward digital assets, represents a significant addition to Regent Law's faculty expertise in financial markets and regulatory policy.

According to reports, the appointment strengthens Regent Law's capabilities in several key areas including financial markets, administrative governance, digital assets, and public policy.
