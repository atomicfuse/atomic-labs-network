The United States has launched sweeping sanctions targeting Chinese "teapot" refineries and shipping networks that facilitate covert oil trade between China and Iran, according to reports. ## Understanding the Shadow Trade Network

The sanctions specifically target the infrastructure and intermediaries that enable what officials describe as a shadow economy between the two nations. According to reports, these "teapot" refineries - smaller, independent Chinese oil processing facilities - have been purchasing Iranian oil through sophisticated evasion networks that include specialized shipping fleets designed to circumvent international monitoring.

The enforcement action encompasses both the refinery operations and the maritime networks that transport the oil, suggesting a comprehensive approach to dismantling the trade infrastructure that has allowed this commerce to continue despite existing sanctions.
