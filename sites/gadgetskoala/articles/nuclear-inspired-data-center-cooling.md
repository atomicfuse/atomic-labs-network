Ferveret's revolutionary Adaptive Phase Cooling relies on a water-free, liquid immersion technique. Unlike conventional air cooling or bulky liquid systems, it creates smaller and more efficient bubbles that enhance heat transfer. This system is designed to improve compute efficiency while significantly reducing energy waste.

### The Science Behind It
