## Smart Chemistry Meets AI
At the forefront of this revolution is the team at IMDEA Materials Institute, who have designed a groundbreaking AI model that predicts the flame resistance of materials, particularly epoxy resins. But how does this work?

### Data-Driven Predictions
The model utilizes data derived from phosphorus-based flame-retardant composites. By analyzing this data, the AI can accurately forecast critical fire-safety indicators based on testing standards like UL-94 and LOI (Limiting Oxygen Index). This means that materials engineers can receive valuable insights into how safe a new material is at lightning speed, rather than enduring the lengthy process of traditional lab testing.

### What Are UL-94 and LOI?
For those not in the know, UL-94 is a standard used to measure the vertical burning behavior of plastic materials. The grades it offers—from V0 to V2—indicate how quickly a material will ignite and how long it will burn. LOI, on the other hand, measures the minimum concentration of oxygen required to sustain combustion. Higher LOI values indicate better flame resistance.
