Corsair, a renowned name in the gaming industry, recently announced its latest collaboration with Call of Duty: Warzone. This partnership has led to the creation of a new range of high-performance gaming equipment designed to enhance the gaming experience. The Corsair Warzone Collection promises to bring a new level of immersion and performance to the popular battle royale game.

The product line-up includes gaming peripherals such as keyboards, mice, mousepads, headsets, and PC components — all decked out in a Warzone-themed design featuring Call of Duty's iconic imagery.

## A Closer Look at the Corsair Warzone Collection

The range kicks off with the **K70 RGB TKL Champion Series Keyboard**. This keyboard features a tenkeyless design for better gaming ergonomics, a tournament switch, and Cherry MX Red mechanical key switches for a fast, precise response.

Next up is the **Sabre RGB Pro Champion Series Mouse**. Lightweight and agile, this mouse offers a high-performance 8000Hz hyper-polling rate — the perfect tool for gamers needing quick, precise movements.

For those who crave immersive audio, the **HS70 Pro Wireless Gaming Headset** is the top choice. It offers high-quality sound reproduction and a fully detachable microphone for clear communication during intense gaming sessions.

The Corsair Warzone Collection also includes the **MP600 Pro XT PCIe 4.0 NVMe M.2 SSD** and the **RM750x power supply**, both ensuring optimal performance and stability for your gaming rig.

Finally, the **MM700 RGB Extended Mouse Pad** provides a solid foundation for your gaming peripherals, with its spacious, low-friction surface and dynamic, customizable RGB lighting.
