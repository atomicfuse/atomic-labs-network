The wait is almost over for millions of fans worldwide. Pre-orders for **Grand Theft Auto VI** are officially set to begin on **June 25**, with digital storefronts and select retailers preparing to take orders for one of the most anticipated game releases in recent memory.

## Pre-Orders Go Live June 25

Rockstar's highly anticipated title will be available to pre-order starting June 25 across major digital platforms. According to reports, both the **PlayStation Store** and the **Microsoft Store** are among the confirmed digital storefronts where pre-orders will be available, alongside select physical retailers.

For fans who don't want to miss the moment pre-orders go live, both the PlayStation Store and Microsoft Store are already offering wishlist functionality for GTA VI. Adding the game to your wishlist on either platform will trigger an alert the moment pre-orders open — a handy way to stay ahead of what is expected to be significant demand.

## Why This Pre-Order Date Is a Big Deal

Grand Theft Auto VI is widely regarded as one of the most eagerly awaited game releases in years. The franchise's massive global following means that even a pre-order announcement carries significant weight across the gaming community, content creators, and retail partners alike.

## How to Add GTA VI to Your Wishlist Right Now

If you're on PlayStation, here's how to make sure you're first in line:

1. Open the **PlayStation Store** on your PS5 or via browser.
2. Search for **Grand Theft Auto VI**.
3. Select **Add to Wishlist** on the game's listing page.
4. You'll receive a notification when pre-orders go live on June 25.

For Xbox players using the Microsoft Store:

1. Head to the **Microsoft Store** on your Xbox console or PC.
2. Search for **Grand Theft Auto VI**.
3. Hit **Wishlist** to save the game to your library watchlist.
4. Watch for an alert when pre-orders officially open.

This simple step ensures you won't miss out when pre-orders drop, especially if limited special editions or bonuses become available.
