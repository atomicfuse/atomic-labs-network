## What Are Cannabis Concentrates?
Cannabis concentrates are products that contain a higher percentage of cannabinoids than traditional flower. These products provide a more potent experience with a smaller serving size. Common types of concentrates include:

### Rosin
Rosin is made by applying heat and pressure to marijuana buds, resulting in a sticky, resinous product. It's prized for being solvent-free and retaining the flavor of the original strain.

### Resin
Unlike rosin, resin involves extracting the active compounds from the plant using solvents. This method can result in higher yields but requires careful processing to remove any residual solvents.

### RSO (Rick Simpson Oil)
This full-spectrum oil is known for its medical benefits and is often used in therapeutic settings. It's a more potent option designed for those who want to maximize their cannabinoid intake.

### Live Resin
Live resin is made from fresh flowers that have been frozen immediately after harvest. This method preserves terpenes, offering a flavorful experience that closely resembles the original plant profile.

## Overcoming Consumer Hesitation
While concentrates offer many advantages, the primary barrier to their widespread adoption is a general lack of knowledge. Many consumers are still intimidated by terms like "dabbing," and confusion about how to consume these products can lead to hesitation. Here are a few strategies to break down these barriers:

### Education is Key
1. **Explainer Guides**: Create accessible content that demystifies cannabis concentrates, explaining their differences and uses. Make this information available in dispensaries and online platforms.
2. **How to Dab**: Provide beginner's guides to dabbing, addressing common misconceptions like the need for complex setups. Highlight low-barrier methods for consumption, such as using simple rigs or portable devices.
3. **Budtender Training**: Foster relationships with budtenders who can explain the benefits and nuances of concentrates, guiding customers towards products suitable for their needs.
