Nintendo continues to delight fans by expanding its Nintendo Switch Online collection, recently adding three notable games to its Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) library. For fans of classic gaming, this update is particularly exciting because it includes a title that has never been released outside of Japan before.

## New Additions to the SNES Library

The latest update to the SNES lineup includes "Super Mario Bros: The Lost Levels," "Kirby's Avalanche," and "Fighter's History." These games bring a mix of platforming, puzzle, and fighting genres to the service, ensuring there's something for everyone to enjoy. The addition of these titles showcases Nintendo's commitment to offering a broad range of experiences for its subscribers.

### Super Mario Bros: The Lost Levels

Among the newly added games, "Super Mario Bros: The Lost Levels" stands out as a significant release. Originally launched in Japan as part of "Super Mario Bros. 2," this game offers a much more challenging experience compared to its predecessor. It's known for its difficult level design and is now available for the first time to players outside Japan, making it a must-play for those seeking a true test of their platforming skills.

### Kirby's Avalanche

"Kirby's Avalanche" introduces a delightful yet competitive puzzle experience. Known in Japan as "Kirby's Ghost Trap," this game challenges players to match falling blobs by color to clear the screen and advance to the next level. Known for its vibrant graphics and engaging mechanics, it is sure to entertain both new players and those revisiting this classic.

### Fighter's History

Rounding out the list is "Fighter's History," a fighting game that offers a nostalgic trip back to the arcade era. Players can choose from a diverse roster of characters, each with unique moves and abilities. The game is noted for its competitive gameplay and classic fighting game elements, making it a great addition for fans of the genre.

## Enhancing the Nintendo Switch Online Experience

Nintendo Switch Online has been steadily growing its library, offering members access to a wide range of classic NES and SNES games. This service not only provides a platform to play these nostalgic titles but also includes features like online multiplayer, save data cloud backup, and exclusive offers. The continuous expansion of its game library is a testament to Nintendo's dedication to enhancing the value of its online service.

### Why These Additions Matter

Adding "Super Mario Bros: The Lost Levels" to the international market is particularly important as it bridges a historical gap for many Mario fans. Meanwhile, "Kirby's Avalanche" and "Fighter's History" diversify the service's offerings, appealing to fans of different gaming genres. This variety ensures that Nintendo Switch Online remains a compelling choice for gamers who appreciate both nostalgic and challenging experiences.

## Looking Forward

With these new additions, Nintendo continues to leverage its rich gaming history to attract and retain subscribers. The anticipation now turns to which games might be added next, as fans eagerly await future announcements. The inclusion of lesser-known titles alongside popular classics is a strategy that keeps the service fresh and engaging.

Nintendo's latest update to the SNES library on Switch Online is a testament to its ongoing commitment to deliver value and nostalgia to its subscribers. By introducing games like "Super Mario Bros: The Lost Levels" to an international audience, alongside beloved titles like "Kirby's Avalanche" and "Fighter's History," Nintendo ensures that its service remains vibrant and appealing to a diverse audience. As the library continues to grow, fans can look forward to even more exciting additions in the future.
