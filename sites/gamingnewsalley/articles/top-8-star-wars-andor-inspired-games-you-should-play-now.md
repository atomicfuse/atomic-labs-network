With the anticipation for the Star Wars: Andor series building, fans of the franchise are seeking to immerse themselves in the Star Wars universe. If you're one of them, we've got you covered. Here's our pick of video games that echo the themes and atmosphere of the upcoming show. These recommendations will help you get your Star Wars fix while you wait for the Andor series.

## Engage in Galactic Battles with Star Wars: Battlefront II

Star Wars: Battlefront II is a must-play for any Star Wars aficionado. This game thrusts you into the heart of the Star Wars universe, offering a thrilling mix of ground and space battles. The storyline is rich and varied, allowing players to experience events from different perspectives across the galaxy.

## Explore a Galaxy Far, Far Away in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic

Experience the thrill of exploration in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic. This game lets you traverse the expansive Star Wars universe, encountering new species, discovering ancient secrets, and shaping your destiny as a Jedi or Sith. Its engrossing narrative and deep RPG mechanics make it a classic in the Star Wars gaming canon.

## Experience a Unique Narrative with Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order

Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order offers an engaging narrative that complements its action-packed gameplay. As a young Jedi on the run, you'll face the Empire's relentless hunt while trying to complete your Jedi training. This game's story-driven adventure and intense lightsaber combat will surely captivate you.

## Immerse Yourself in Star Wars: Squadrons

Star Wars: Squadrons puts you in the pilot's seat, giving you control over iconic Star Wars spacecraft. Experience thrilling dogfights in space and immerse yourself in the game's immersive storyline that spans both the New Republic and the crumbling Galactic Empire.

In conclusion, these Star Wars-themed video games will transport you into the universe you love, offering diverse experiences and stories to keep you engaged. Whether you're a Jedi at heart or lean towards the dark side, there's a game out there for you. So, buckle up, get your blaster ready, and prepare to explore the galaxy before Andor arrives.
