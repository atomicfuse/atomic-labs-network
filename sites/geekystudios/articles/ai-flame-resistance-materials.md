## What’s Cooking in AI? 
### The Power of Prediction
The AI tool in question leverages comprehensive data from phosphorus-based flame-retardant composites. By forecasting key fire-safety indicators such as UL-94 and LOI, it categorizes the performance of materials in a way that is clear and understandable. But what do these indicators mean?  
- **UL-94** is a standard that measures the flammability of plastic materials, categorized by how quickly they extinguish after being ignited.
- **LOI (Limiting Oxygen Index)** indicates the minimum concentration of oxygen needed for a material to sustain combustion, helping define how flammable a material is in various environments.

### The Shift from Trial-and-Error to AI
Traditionally employed methods of developing flame-retardant materials involve the slow and often inefficient process of lab trials. This new AI approach is a game changer, making it quicker and cheaper to screen polymers that can withstand intense flames. With safety demands skyrocketing, especially in sectors like construction and transportation, this model offers a much-needed shortcut.
