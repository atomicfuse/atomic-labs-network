## Meet the AI Model Revolutionizing Flame Resistance Testing

Researchers at the IMDEA Materials Institute have developed an innovative AI model capable of accurately predicting the flame resistance of new epoxy resins. By leveraging data from phosphorus-based flame-retardant composites, this tool forecasts essential fire-safety indicators, specifically UL-94 and LOI ratings, that materials engineers heavily rely on.

### What Are UL-94 and LOI Anyway?

Before we dive deeper, let’s break down those acronyms. 
- **UL-94**: This is a standard from Underwriters Laboratories that measures how materials behave when exposed to fire, specifically how soon they extinguish after a flame is removed. 
- **LOI (Limiting Oxygen Index)**: This indicates the minimum concentration of oxygen needed to sustain combustion. Higher LOI values mean greater flame resistance.

These metrics are more than just numbers; they inform industries on which materials can be safely used in electronics, EV batteries, aerospace interiors, and various construction materials.
