## Peak Season for Swedish Getaways

As Midsummer travel to Scandinavia reaches its peak in June, travelers seeking authentic Swedish experiences have a curated selection of nine stylish Airbnb rentals to choose from, according to a recent Vogue selection. These properties span across Sweden, each offering distinct experiences designed to capture the essence of Swedish summer and the country's famous extended daylight hours.

## Coastal and Waterfront Retreats

For those drawn to Sweden's stunning coastline, several waterfront options provide the perfect seaside escape. The Österlen House, priced at $157 per night, offers travelers a quintessential seaside stay experience. Similarly, Kungshamn, also at $157 per night, features contemporary waterfront design that embodies modern Scandinavian aesthetics.

The Aurora Isle, available for $183 per night, takes seclusion to the next level as a private island cabin. This unique property offers true isolation for travelers seeking an off-the-grid experience, complete with no electricity to fully disconnect from modern life.

## Historic Properties with Character

History enthusiasts will find compelling options among the curated selections. Gamla Kassan, priced at $185 per night, specifically caters to history buffs seeking accommodations with a story to tell. The Beautiful Chapel presents an extraordinary opportunity to stay in a renovated 1912 Baptist church located on the island of Gotland, offering guests a unique blend of historic architecture and modern comfort.

In Nyköping, travelers can experience authentic Swedish heritage at the Charming Cottage, an 18th-century property that provides a glimpse into Sweden's architectural past.

## Design-Forward and Stockholm-Adjacent Options

For design enthusiasts, Blombergsgården, priced at $164 per night, serves as inspiration for those seeking Scandinavian design elements in their accommodations. The property selection emphasizes the clean lines and functional beauty that Swedish design is known for worldwide.

Travelers wanting proximity to Sweden's capital have multiple options. The House with a Fantastic View, the premium option at $334 per night, offers convenient access to Stockholm while maintaining a retreat-like atmosphere. For those seeking a balance between urban access and peaceful surroundings, the Cosy House provides accommodation outside Stockholm's center.

## The Midsummer Experience

These properties are particularly well-suited for experiencing Swedish Midsummer, one of the country's most celebrated cultural traditions. The extended summer daylight hours that characterize Swedish summers create unique opportunities for travelers to maximize their experiences, whether exploring coastal areas, historic sites, or simply enjoying the distinctive light that defines Nordic summers.
