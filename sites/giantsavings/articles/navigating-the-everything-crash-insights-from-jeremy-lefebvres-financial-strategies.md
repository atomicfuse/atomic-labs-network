## Understanding the 'Everything Crash'

The term 'everything crash' refers to a scenario where multiple asset classes experience significant declines simultaneously. This can include stocks, real estate, and other investments, leading to widespread financial uncertainty.

## Strategies to Mitigate Risks

Lefebvre emphasizes the importance of diversification in mitigating risks during such crashes. By spreading investments across different sectors, the impact of a downturn in any one area can be reduced.
