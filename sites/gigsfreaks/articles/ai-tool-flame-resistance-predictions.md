## The Mechanics Behind the AI Tool
### How it Works
The AI model utilizes data from phosphorus-based flame-retardant composites. By analyzing vast datasets, it forecasts crucial fire safety indicators like UL-94 and LOI. ### Understanding UL-94 and LOI
For those unfamiliar with these technical terms:
- **UL-94**: This safety standard classifies materials by their flammability characteristics (like HB, V-0, and V-1). The more resistant a material is to combustion, the higher its classification.
- **LOI (Limiting Oxygen Index)**: This measures the minimum concentration of oxygen needed for a material to sustain combustion. Higher LOI values indicate better flame resistance.

These metrics are essential for ensuring the safety of products in high-risk applications.
