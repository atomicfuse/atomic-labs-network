## The Rare Condition and Its Challenges

This condition presents significant challenges, as it exposes the heart to potential damage and infection, making survival beyond birth extremely rare. Historically, the prognosis for infants born with ectopia cordis has been grim, with very few surviving past infancy. This made the birth of this particular child a critical case that demanded immediate and innovative medical solutions.
