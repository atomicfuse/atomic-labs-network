That afternoon energy crash hits us all. You know the feeling—lunch is done, you've caught up on office gossip, and suddenly you're counting down the minutes until 5 PM. When that slump strikes, there's one reliable solution: a Starbucks run. And with their fresh spring menu dropping five brand-new beverages, you've got even more reasons to treat yourself to that pick-me-up.

![Four cups of beverages held in a carrier, with people in the background.](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod/images/e2f6e0ea-c6c8-4fcd-94d3-10c2822feaac.jpg?crop=0.75xw:1xh;center,top&resize=640:*)

The new lineup launched on Monday, April 6th, and it's all about mango madness. The star attractions are three customizable Refreshers: the Mango Strawberry Refresher, Mango Strawberry Lemonade Refresher, and the Mango Dream (which swaps water for coconut milk). Here's the cool part—you can order any of them caffeine-free, with the standard 25 milligrams of caffeine, or pump it up to 100 milligrams as an Energy Refresher.

But wait, there's more! Starbucks also introduced an Iced Mango Cream Chai and an Iced Mango Cream Matcha to round out the seasonal offerings. Being the dedicated food enthusiast I am, I headed straight to my local Starbucks to put them all to the test.

Here's the honest breakdown of what's worth your hard-earned cash.

## The Refresher Trio: Light, Bright, and Surprisingly Good

### Mango Strawberry Refresher

![Hand holding a cup of iced beverage.](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod/images/1a383c4d-e79c-4f23-8dce-b72c2872f9f5.jpg?crop=0.804xw:0.603xh;0.14xw,0.243xh&resize=980:*)

My first sip completely changed my perspective on Refreshers. How had I been overlooking this category? This drink is everything you want in an afternoon pick-me-up—light, juicy, and refreshing without crossing into sugar-bomb territory. The mango plays beautifully with the strawberries' gentle tartness, and the real fruit pieces give it a fresh finish that prevents any artificial aftertaste.

### Mango Strawberry Lemonade Refresher

![Brightly colored drink held in hand near a window.](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod/images/b06c6645-25ae-451d-b8fb-51fca5fe552d.jpg?crop=0.875xw:0.656xh;0.083xw,0.234xh&resize=980:*)

This version takes the original formula and cranks up the brightness. The lemonade addition brings a sweeter, more vibrant edge that makes the mango and strawberry flavors really pop. If the regular version is your chill, everyday option, this is the one you'll crave on scorching hot days when you need something with more personality.

### Mango Dream - The Clear Winner

![A hand holding a drink with a handwritten message on the cup.](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod/images/cca125bb-f140-4d88-a866-025acc91464d.jpg?crop=0.853xw:0.64xh;0.121xw,0.277xh&resize=980:*)

This was my absolute favorite of the trio, and honestly, the drink I'd rush back for first. The Mango Dream takes the Strawberry Mango Refresher base and adds coconut milk, transforming it into something that tastes like a proper beach vacation in a cup. The coconut milk creates a creamy richness that softens the fruity sweetness, making each sip incredibly smooth and satisfying. It's the perfect balance of tropical flavors without being overwhelming.

## The Cream Collection: Surprisingly Sophisticated

### Iced Mango Cream Chai

![Hand holding a decorated green beverage with two other drinks in the background.](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod/images/464c16ec-bd2f-48ad-937c-53c4436c55c0.jpg?crop=0.872xw:0.654xh;0.079xw,0.323xh&resize=980:*)

Chai lovers, this one's for you! While chai typically gets most of its love during fall and winter months, this iced mango cream version makes a compelling case for year-round enjoyment. You still get those familiar warming spices—cinnamon, clove, and cardamom—but the sweet mango cream lightens everything up, creating a drink that's perfect for warmer weather without losing that cozy chai character.

### Iced Mango Cream Matcha

![Iced beverage in a clear cup held against a window.](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod/images/08151504-607c-4070-a952-9a3b032ccb40.jpg?crop=0.869xw:0.652xh;0.114xw,0.223xh&resize=980:*)

This combination surprised me the most. Matcha and mango might sound like an odd couple, but they work incredibly well together. The earthy, slightly bitter notes of matcha get beautifully balanced by the creamy mango addition, which smooths out some of the grassier edges that can sometimes make matcha intimidating for newcomers. The result is sophisticated and layered without being too sweet or heavy.

## The Verdict: What's Worth Your Money

If I had to rank these five drinks, the Mango Dream takes the top spot hands down. It's creamy, tropical, and refreshing—basically everything you want in a spring beverage. The Iced Mango Cream Matcha comes in second for its unexpected harmony of flavors, followed closely by the Iced Mango Cream Chai for chai enthusiasts looking to switch things up.

The two standard Refreshers are solid choices too, especially if you prefer lighter, less creamy drinks. The lemonade version edges out the original for its extra brightness, but both are refreshing options that won't disappoint.

With customizable caffeine levels across the Refresher lineup, these drinks work whether you need a gentle afternoon lift or a serious energy boost. Plus, at a time when many of us are looking for lighter, more refreshing alternatives to heavy coffee drinks, Starbucks' spring menu delivers exactly what we're craving.

So next time that mid-afternoon slump hits, skip your usual order and give one of these mango-infused creations a try. Your taste buds—and your energy levels—will thank you.
