New Riff Bottled-in-Bond Kentucky Straight Bourbon has claimed the prestigious title of World's Best Bourbon at the 2026 World Whiskies Awards, beating out countless premium competitors in two rigorous rounds of judging. What makes this victory even sweeter? You can find this award-winning bottle in all 50 states for around $40.

## What Makes This Bourbon So Special?

New Riff's winning bourbon follows a classic recipe that's been perfected over time. The distillery crafts this exceptional spirit from a thoughtfully balanced mash bill of 65% corn, 30% rye, and 5% malted barley. That higher rye content gives the bourbon its distinctive spicy character that judges clearly appreciated.

As a bottled-in-bond whiskey, this bourbon meets strict federal standards that guarantee quality and authenticity. It's aged for at least four years in new charred American oak barrels and bottled at exactly 100 proof – no more, no less. Like all New Riff products, it's bottled without chill filtration, preserving the full flavor profile that nature intended.

## A Taste Profile Worth Celebrating

The World Whiskies Awards judges were clearly impressed by what they found in their glasses. Their tasting notes paint a picture of complexity and balance, starting with bright aromas of lemon zest and butterscotch that draw you in.

On the palate, this bourbon delivers layers of flavor that unfold beautifully. Expect warm cinnamon and rich caramel alongside fresh fig and vanilla cookie notes. The bourbon's character deepens with hints of sweet pastry, dried leather, pumpernickel rye, and even subtle notes of corned beef and brine that add savory complexity.

The finish brings everything together with a warm, sweet conclusion featuring caramel, lemon peel, crumb cookie, and coffee notes that linger pleasantly.

## The New Riff Story

Founded in 2014 in Newport, Kentucky, New Riff has quickly established itself as a serious player in the craft distilling world. While the distillery is known for experimenting with unique grain recipes – including balboa rye, red turkey wheat, and 100% malted rye – their flagship bottled-in-bond bourbon proves that sometimes the classics done right can't be beaten.

What's particularly impressive is how accessible this award-winning bourbon remains. While many craft distilleries struggle with limited distribution, New Riff has managed to get their products into all 50 states – a rare achievement for an independently owned operation.

## Double Victory for New Riff

New Riff didn't just win the overall World's Best Bourbon title – they also took home the "Best Kentucky Bourbon" award, further cementing their place among the Bluegrass State's finest distilleries. This double recognition speaks to the consistent quality they're achieving.
