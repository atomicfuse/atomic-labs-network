## Gravitational Waves: The Universe’s Soundtrack
Before diving into the findings, let’s talk about how we’re able to 'hear' black holes. Gravitational waves are ripples in spacetime caused by massive events, like the merging of black holes. When two black holes collide, they send out waves that travel across the universe, much like how a pebble creates ripples in a pond. These waves are crucial for scientists as they provide a way to study objects that are otherwise invisible and elusive.
