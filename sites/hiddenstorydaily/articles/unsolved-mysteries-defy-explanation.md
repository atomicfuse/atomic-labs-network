Using modern scanning technology can unlock ages-old mysteries, and LiDAR has resurrected interest in the ancient site of Ohio's Great Serpent Mound. This mysterious earthwork exhibits astonishing precision, leading experts to ponder its origins and purposes.

### What Does LiDAR Reveal?
LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) offers a unique glimpse into the site's layout and construction, raising tantalizing theories about who might have built it and why. The true purpose of this ancient mound remains a subject of intense debate among archaeologists.

## 7. The Mystery of the Hessdalen Lights
In Norway’s Hessdalen Valley, eerie lights appear in the sky with no definitive explanation. These bright orbs have puzzled scientists for decades, prompting discussions about alien activity and plasma physics. 

## 8. The Bermuda Triangle
The enigmatic Bermuda Triangle has long been the stuff of legends, with countless aircraft and ships disappearing without a trace. Is the phenomenon linked to underwater cities, extraterrestrial involvement, or just bad weather?

## 9. The Wow! Signal
In 1977, astronomer Jerry R. Ehman discovered a 72-second radio signal that appeared to originate from outer space. To this day, no one knows the source of the Wow! Signal, leading seekers of extraterrestrial communication to wonder if it was a sign or simply an anomaly.

## 10. The Nazca Lines
Carved into the desert in Peru, the Nazca Lines present enormous geoglyphs that can only be fully appreciated from the air. The purpose remains unclear—were they made for deities, astronomical calendars, or extraterrestrial visitors?

## 11. The Dyatlov Pass Incident
In 1959, a group of skilled hikers in Russia met an inexplicable fate when they were found dead under mysterious circumstances. The bizarre injuries and strange footprints in the snow continue to fuel conspiracy theories, including government experiments and supernatural lore.
