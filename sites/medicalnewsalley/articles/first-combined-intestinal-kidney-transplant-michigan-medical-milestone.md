## Breaking New Ground in Transplant Medicine

Briana Dery of Twining, Michigan, has made medical history as the first person in the state to receive a combined intestinal and kidney transplant in a single operation. At 33 years old, Dery's groundbreaking surgery represents a significant milestone in transplant medicine and offers new hope for patients facing complex organ failure.

According to reports, the 10-hour procedure was performed by a coordinated team at Henry Ford Health, marking a rare achievement in the field of transplant surgery. Combined intestinal-kidney transplants are exceptionally uncommon, with only 56 such procedures ever performed in the United States, making Dery's case particularly noteworthy.
