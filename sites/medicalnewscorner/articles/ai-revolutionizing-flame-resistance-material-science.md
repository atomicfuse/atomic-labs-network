## The Power of Prediction
### Making the Complex Simple
At the heart of this AI model lies a sophisticated predictive capability that utilizes data from phosphorus-based flame-retardant composites. This means that instead of relying on traditional lab tests—which can be slow and costly—the AI can assess the fire safety indicators of epoxy resins much more rapidly. Notably, it forecasts UL-94 (a standard for flammability) and LOI (Limiting Oxygen Index) metrics, translating these technical results into clear, actionable categories for materials engineers.

## Understanding Fire Safety Metrics
### What do UL-94 and LOI Mean?
For those who might not be familiar, UL-94 and LOI are crucial standards in the fire safety spectrum. UL-94 assesses the flammability of plastic materials, categorizing them from V-0 (the best) to V-2, based on their ability to extinguish flames. LOI, on the other hand, measures the minimum concentration of oxygen needed to sustain combustion—you want this number to be as low as possible for optimal safety. The AI model developed by IMDEA correlates these metrics effectively with the material's performance, making the whole process clearer for engineers.
