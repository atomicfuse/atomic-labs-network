## Community Wellness Takes Center Stage

Oyster Bay Town Supervisor Joseph Saladino has announced that registration will soon open for the Town's Pilates in the Park program, bringing accessible outdoor fitness to the community this summer. The 10-week Saturday fitness series promises to combine wellness with the natural beauty of Theodore Roosevelt Park.

## Program Details and Schedule

According to reports, the Pilates in the Park program will begin July 11th and run for 10 consecutive weeks. Sessions are scheduled for Saturday mornings from 10:00 to 11:00 a.m. at Theodore Roosevelt Park, providing residents with a consistent weekly wellness routine in an outdoor setting.

The timing appears strategically chosen to take advantage of pleasant summer weather while offering a convenient weekend fitness option for busy community members.

## Benefits of Outdoor Pilates Practice

Pilates offers numerous wellness benefits as a low-impact exercise form that focuses on core strength, flexibility, and mind-body connection. When practiced outdoors, participants can enjoy additional benefits including fresh air, natural vitamin D from sunlight, and the mental health boost that comes from connecting with nature.

The park setting at Theodore Roosevelt Park provides an ideal backdrop for mindful movement, allowing participants to engage with their local environment while prioritizing personal wellness goals.
