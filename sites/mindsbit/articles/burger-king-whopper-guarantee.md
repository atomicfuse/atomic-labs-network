## What is the Whopper Guarantee?
The Whopper Guarantee is Burger King’s promise to replace any **unsatisfactory Whoppers** for free. Additionally, customers will receive a **free Whopper on their next visit** if they experience an issue with their order. This is a significant step in addressing customer dissatisfaction and ensuring that each guest receives the quality they expect from the brand.

## How Does It Work?
When you feel your Whopper doesn't meet expectations, simply inform the staff about your concerns. They are prepared to remake your burger at no charge. Moreover, when this situation arises, you will receive a **QR code** that you can scan during your next visit to claim your free Whopper. This digital approach streamlines the redemption process and encourages repeat visits.
