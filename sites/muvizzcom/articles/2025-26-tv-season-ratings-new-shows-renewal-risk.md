## Why Demo Ratings Matter More Than Raw Numbers

According to industry analysis, advertiser-valued demographic ratings carry significantly more weight than total audience numbers when networks evaluate renewal prospects. This metric focuses on viewers aged 18-49, the demographic most coveted by advertisers and therefore most valuable to network revenue streams.

This emphasis on demo performance over total viewership explains why some shows with larger overall audiences might face cancellation while others with smaller but more demographically desirable audiences receive renewals. For networks operating in an increasingly competitive streaming landscape, attracting the right audience segments has become more crucial than ever.
