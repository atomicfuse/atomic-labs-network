## A Historic Farewell in Wine Country

The world's first LGBTQ-focused wine festival is pouring its final glasses this summer. According to reports, Queer Wine Fest will hold its last celebration on June 28, 2026, in McMinnville, Oregon's renowned Willamette Valley, marking the end of a five-year journey that transformed representation in the wine industry.

Founder Remy Drabkin has announced plans to conclude the pioneering event after achieving its original mission to uplift the queer wine community and support LGBTQ vintners. The decision comes as the festival celebrates a legacy of growth and influence that extends far beyond its Oregon origins.
