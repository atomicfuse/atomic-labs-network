## Movie Magic Meets Fast Food

Wendy's is rolling out a limited-time Minions & Monsters meal promotion tied to Illumination's upcoming film, bringing themed dining options to families nationwide. The promotion features both kids' and adult meal options, exclusive collectible toys, and introduces a brand-new Banana Frosty Swirl flavor.

## Kids' Meal Options Now Available

The kids' meal portion of the promotion is already available at participating locations. Young diners can choose from four main options: 2-piece chicken tenders, 4-piece chicken nuggets, hamburger, or cheeseburger. Each meal comes with a drink and the choice of junior fries or apple bites as a side.

What sets this promotion apart are the six exclusive collectible toys featuring characters James, Henry, Ed, Richard Goomi, and Dort from the upcoming film. These collectibles are designed to encourage repeat visits as families try to collect all available characters.

## Adult Meals Launch Mid-June

Starting June 15, Wendy's will expand the promotion to include adult meal options. These themed meals offer a choice between the Big Bacon Classic or Spicy Chicken Sandwich, accompanied by small fries and a small portion of the new Banana Frosty Swirl.

Adult diners will also have access to four exclusive blind box Minions & Monsters collectibles, adding an element of surprise and collecting appeal for grown-up fans of the franchise.

## New Banana Frosty Swirl Debuts

The centerpiece of the adult meal promotion is Wendy's new Banana Frosty Swirl, a limited-time dessert that combines banana cream sauce swirled into the chain's classic Vanilla Frosty base. This represents a departure from traditional Frosty flavors and aims to capture the tropical, playful spirit of the Minions franchise.

## Specialty Beverages Join the Fun

The promotion extends beyond food items to include two new Coca-Cola Freestyle beverage options. The Pineapple Minion MischieFizz and Goomi's Glimey Lime offer themed drink choices that complement the meal experience and tie into the movie's characters and aesthetic.

## Strategic Summer Timing

According to reports, movie tie-in promotions like this one are strategically timed to drive foot traffic and create social media buzz during the competitive summer blockbuster season. The collectible element creates additional incentive for repeat visits, which is particularly valuable as Wendy's focuses on value offerings to boost sales.
