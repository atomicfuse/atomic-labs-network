### Hack 1: The Hairspray Shield

According to reports, using hairspray on collars serves as an effective protective barrier against makeup transfer. This preventative measure creates a protective layer that helps repel potential stains before they can set into the fabric.

Makeup setting sprays aren't just for your face—they play a crucial role in preventing clothing stains. The experts recommend incorporating setting sprays into your makeup routine as a dual-purpose solution that keeps both your makeup and your whites looking perfect.

### Hack 3: Sodium Percarbonate Power

For laundry care, sodium percarbonate emerges as a key ingredient for maintaining white clothing. This laundry booster helps tackle stains and keep whites bright without the need for expensive dry cleaning services.
