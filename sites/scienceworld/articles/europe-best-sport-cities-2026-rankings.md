## Marbella Tops European Sport City Rankings

European Best Destinations has released its ranking of the 10 best sport cities in Europe for 2026, with Marbella claiming the top spot ahead of Monaco, Oslo, and Paris. The rankings evaluated cities based on infrastructure, international events, year-round active lifestyle potential, and inclusivity.

## Sports Tourism Market Explodes

The timing of these rankings aligns with explosive growth in sports tourism, which is projected to reach $1.3 trillion by 2032, according to reports. European cities are capitalizing on this trend by blending elite competitions, world-class facilities, and accessible everyday sports culture.

The selection criteria went beyond traditional stadium experiences. According to European Best Destinations, the chosen cities excel at offering opportunities where visitors can "watch sport, practice sport and live sport all year round."

## Key Highlights from Top Cities

Oslo's inclusion reflects the city's commitment to women's sports advancement, with the Norwegian capital hosting the UEFA Women's Champions League Final. Paris leverages its post-Olympic legacy from recent games, while Nice showcases cycling culture as host to the Tour de France finale.

## Urban Design Meets Athletic Culture

The rankings highlight a broader shift from stadium-only sports experiences to lifestyle-integrated urban design. Cities are creating environments where athletic activities seamlessly blend into daily life, from running routes along scenic waterfronts to year-round outdoor fitness facilities.

This evolution reflects changing traveler preferences, as sports tourism reshapes destination selection criteria. The ranked cities demonstrate how combining athletic achievement opportunities, wellness infrastructure, and travel appeal creates compelling destinations for the growing active lifestyle market.
