## Critical Shipping Route Under Pressure

According to reports, US forces have boarded a ship carrying Iranian oil, while the Trump administration has issued orders for the navy to shoot any vessel laying mines in the strategic waterway.

## New Transit Fee System Raises Stakes

Iran has simultaneously implemented a new "transit toll" system in the Strait of Hormuz, denominated in rials according to parliamentary plans. Reports indicate Iran has already received its first revenue from these controversial fees, marking a significant shift in how the critical shipping lane operates.
