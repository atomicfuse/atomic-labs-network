The club, co-owned by Hollywood stars Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney, has ambitions that match its newfound fame. This includes a bold plan to bring a seasoned Premier League talent into their ranks, reflecting their commitment to ascend the English football ladder.

The Welsh club, currently competing in the lower leagues, is looking at Scotland's Andy Robertson, a top-tier left-back known for his time with Liverpool FC. Robertson's skills have made him one of the standout defenders in the Premier League, and his potential move would be a significant coup for Wrexham. This ambition highlights the club's desire to build a squad capable of challenging at higher levels and fulfilling their Premier League aspirations.
