Among the phenomena reported on Alor Island are the mysterious 'watchers'—figures that appear unexpectedly, leaving witnesses questioning their origins and purpose. The watchers have become a symbol of Alor Island's enigmatic reputation, drawing interest from those intrigued by the unexplained.
