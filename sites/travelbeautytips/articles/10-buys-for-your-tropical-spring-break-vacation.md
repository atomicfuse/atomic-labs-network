## 1. Adriana Degreas Vintage Lily Long Dress With Straps

When humidity and temperatures are soaring, an airy silhouette in earthy tones will always hit. Pull a denim jacket over your shoulders if that tropical breeze picks up.

## 2. PatBO Candy Linen Short

Never overlook the versatility of linen. This high-waisted short is eternally flattering, and the neutral hue works for nearly any beachside occasion.

## 3. Altuzarra Sleary Top

Silk done right aligns well with a vacation wardrobe. Wear this high-neck blouse with a white skort or flowy pants.

## 4. Elie Saab Wedge Sandal

A wedge is completely classic for a warm-weather getaway. This pair proves it's all about the details, with subtle pink highlights and a miniature metallic logo.

## 5. Sensi Studio Medium Picnic Basket

The perfect spring break bag is three things: woven, handheld, and able to pair well with any outfit in your suitcase. This one simply nails them all.

## 6. Altuzarra Lily Dress

A smocked silhouette and 100 percent-cotton construction mean this Altuzarra number will both pack well and wear easily. Pair it with mixed metals to up the glam with a low-key bent.

## 7. Adriana Degreas Vintage Lily Solid Appliqué Strapless Swimsuit

Tan lines be gone! A strapless, timeless cut is never a bad idea when it comes to swimwear, after all.

## 8. Christopher Esber Davies 99 Sunglasses

A stylish pair of shades doesn't need to get overly complicated. Christopher Esber does of-the-moment right, marrying on-trend shapes with classic appeal.

## 9. Larroudé Milan Flat Mule

Black is totally appropriate for an oceanside adventure, so long as the texture is right. Go for a raffia flat with white jeans and a crop top for an easy, chic look.

## 10. Christopher Esber Crystal G String Bikini Brief

Simplicity always has a place. This triangle bikini combines traditional lines with a bold color for a fun, wearable combination.
