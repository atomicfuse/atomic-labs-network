Helena Christensen, the renowned Danish supermodel, shares her go-to beauty routine for a flawless look on an exotic tropical beach night. Her approach combines skincare essentials with expert makeup techniques, offering insights that are both practical and luxurious.

Helena's beauty journey began in her early 20s when she moved to Paris to pursue modeling. Initially a late bloomer with makeup, she quickly learned the importance of skincare due to the heavy makeup demands of her career. Her experiences have shaped a routine that balances effective skincare with elegant makeup choices.

## Skincare Essentials for Radiant Skin

Helena starts her routine with the Silklyfe Silk Soothing Spray, offering hydration and preparation for the skin. This is followed by Dr. Loretta Barrier Enhancing Milky Essence to enhance skin's resilience. The SickScience ShapeShift V-Line Jaw Defining Serum is a key step, helping to sculpt and define facial contours.

For brightening and even skin tone, Helena uses Allies of Skin Tranexamic & Arbutin Advanced Brightening Serum. Moisturization is crucial, with Tata Harper Repairative Moisturizer and Angela Caglia Soufflé Moisturizer providing rich hydration. Protection is non-negotiable; Silklyfe Daily Defense+ Sunscreen shields her skin from harsh UV rays.

## Makeup Techniques for a Stunning Night Look

Transitioning to makeup, Helena opts for Westman Atelier Sun Tone Bronzing Drops to achieve a sun-kissed glow. The Lancôme Teint Idole Matte Foundation offers a flawless base, while Goop Colorblur Glow Balm and Merit Flush Balm Cream Blush add a natural flush to her cheeks.

For her eyes, Helena applies Hourglass Scattered Light Glitter Eyeshadow for a touch of glamour, blending with Merit Brush No. 1 and Brush No. 2 for precision. The final touch, a Dries Van Noten Matte Lipstick, completes her look with a bold, sophisticated finish.

## Reflections on a Glamorous Career

While guiding through her routine, Helena reflects on her first runway experience with haute couture powerhouse Valentino and her initial dreams of becoming a photographer. These memories encapsulate the grandeur and transformative nature of her modeling journey.

Helena Christensen's beach night beauty routine is a testament to her expertise and passion for both skincare and makeup. Her carefully curated steps ensure a radiant, camera-ready look that embodies elegance and sophistication.
