Renowned for her dazzling performances and timeless beauty, Kristin Chenoweth opens up about her beauty routine, secrets, and inspiration. A Broadway sensation and Emmy and Tony award winner, Kristin shares her beauty mantras and the products she can't live without.

## Chenoweth's Beauty Routine

Chenoweth's day commences with a skin care regimen that revolves around hydration. Opting for lighter moisturizers in the morning and heavier ones at night, she never skimps on moisturizing. She is a devoted fan of La Mer, particularly its eye cream and moisturizing cream, which she considers her beauty staples.

## The Importance of Sunscreen

Aside from moisturizing, Chenoweth also stresses the importance of sunscreen. She uses a tinted sunscreen from EltaMD, which not only provides protection but also adds a touch of color to her complexion. The importance of sunscreen can't be overstated, especially for someone as busy as Chenoweth.

## Makeup Essentials

When it comes to makeup, Chenoweth leans towards natural hues but isn't shy about experimenting with colors. She loves Huda Beauty's eyeshadow palettes for their variety of shades. Her make-up routine is incomplete without Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk Foundation, a product she swears by for a flawless finish.

## Chenoweth's Hair Care

Chenoweth's hair care routine is as meticulous as her skincare. She uses Olaplex products to maintain the health of her hair. She's also a fan of Living Proof's Full Dry Volume Blast, which gives her hair the desired lift and volume.

## Beauty Inspiration

Chenoweth draws her beauty inspiration from various sources. She mentions her admiration for Audrey Hepburn's timeless elegance and Dolly Parton's bold style. Additionally, she admires her mother's natural beauty and credits her for instilling the importance of skin care in her at an early age.

## Final Words on Beauty

Chenoweth's approach to beauty is holistic, emphasizing the importance of feeling good on the inside to shine on the outside. She believes in staying hydrated, getting enough sleep, and maintaining a positive attitude. She's a firm believer that beauty is more than skin deep, and true beauty comes from within.
