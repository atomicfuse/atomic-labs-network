Comedian and Golden Globes host Nikki Glaser shares her beauty secrets in a detailed pre-show routine. Known for her wit and charm, Nikki takes us through her everyday skincare and makeup regimen, focusing on how she prepares for the prestigious Golden Globe Awards.

## Skincare First

Nikki begins her routine with a focus on skincare, emphasizing the importance of a clean and hydrated face. She uses the Ouai Leave In Conditioner and Nutrafol Women's Balance Hair Growth Nutraceutical to ensure her hair and scalp are well-nourished. For her facial cleanse, she opts for the Laneige Water Bank Gentle Gel Cleanser, followed by Plated Skin Science Intense Serum and Eye Serum to maintain a youthful glow. The hydrating effects of Peter Thomas Roth Water Drench Eye Patches and The Ordinary Plant-Derived Squalane are also pivotal in her preparation.

## Makeup Essentials

Transitioning to makeup, Nikki shares her experiences with products like Kosas DreamBeam SPF 40 and IT Cosmetics CC+ Color Correcting Cream SPF 50+. She appreciates the coverage and protection these products offer, especially under the harsh lights of the red carpet. For setting her look, she applies Jouer Soft Focus Hydrate + Set Powder and adds a touch of shine with Dior Forever Glow Powder Highlighter.

## Eye Makeup

Nikki's eye makeup routine involves the Pat McGrath Labs Blitz Astral Eyeshadow Quad and Fenty Beauty Pro Filt'r Soft Matte Longwear Foundation, balanced meticulously with Benefit Cosmetics Gimme Brow+ Volumizing Brow Gel. As she experiments with a winged eyeliner look, she candidly discusses the challenges and joys of perfecting her makeup.

## Color and Contour

Adding a pop of color to her cheeks, Nikki uses E.l.f Cosmetics Putty Blush and Make Up For Ever Artist Color Pencil Extreme, complemented by Ilia Sunshift Cream Bronzer for contouring. For her eyebrows, the Kosas Brow Pop Brow Pencil is a staple in achieving that perfect arch.

## Beyond Products

Nikki's routine isn't just about products — she also incorporates lymphatic brushes into her regimen, highlighting the importance of facial massages for a rejuvenated appearance. Her recent purchase, a mini trampoline, is another fun addition she claims is great for both fitness and mood.

Throughout her routine, Nikki Glaser shares personal anecdotes, including her favorite movie of the year, making this beauty journey as entertaining as it is informative. Her approach is a blend of reliable beauty staples and playful experimentation, reflecting her vibrant personality.
