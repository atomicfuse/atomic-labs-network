### The Tasting Experience
As we prepared our drinks, we couldn’t help but embrace the excitement of trying each one. With fun commentary and a few laughs, we ranked these celebrity cocktails based on taste, originality, and how well they represented the personalities of their creators.

#### 1. Timothy Chalamet’s Rum and Coke
The Rum and Coke may seem like an underwhelming start, but once mixed, it shines through its simplicity. The balance of flavors hinges on quality ingredients—in this case, a solid rum paired with classic cola. Surprisingly, even a celebrity’s endorsement can’t mask the fact that this drink’s appeal lies in nostalgia. **Final ranking: 3/5.**

#### 2. Lionel Messi’s Vino Sprite
Messi’s unconventional choice intrigued us. The combination brought forth a light fizz, making it incredibly thirst-quenching. Each sip was like sipping on summer! While the initial shock value is high, the flavors made for a surprisingly refreshing experience. **Final ranking: 4/5.**

#### 3. Taylor Swift’s French Blonde
Taylor brings her touch of boldness with the French Blonde. This drink, with its bitter twists and elegant flair, divided our taste buds. Some of us adored the complex mix, while others couldn’t get past the prominent citrus notes. In the end, it’s a drink that thrives on personal preference. **Final ranking: 3.5/5.**

#### 4. Robert Pattinson’s Michelada
Last but not least, we dived into the Michelada. With its spicy tomato base and zesty lime contrast, it turns out to be a foray into bold flavors. Perfect for fans of a good kick, it also felt like a great drink for a weekend brunch get-together. **Final ranking: 5/5.**
