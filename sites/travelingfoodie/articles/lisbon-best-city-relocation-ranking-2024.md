## Lisbon Claims Top Spot in New Relocation Study

A comprehensive new ranking by Global Citizen Solutions has named Lisbon as the world's best city for relocation, according to reports. The Portuguese capital outranked major international destinations by excelling across multiple categories that matter most to those considering a move abroad.

The study evaluated cities based on crucial factors including safety, health care quality, affordability, air quality, mobility options, English proficiency, and social integration opportunities. These metrics reflect the practical realities that expatriates and digital nomads face when choosing their next home base.

## The Complete Top 10 Rankings

According to the Global Citizen Solutions index, the world's best relocation destinations are:

1. Lisbon
2. Amsterdam
3. Melbourne
4. Vienna
5. Barcelona
6. Singapore
7. Auckland
8. Tokyo
9. Copenhagen
10. Seoul

Tel Aviv, despite its appeal to many international residents, ranked 17th overall in the comprehensive study. The Israeli city was also highlighted among the most expensive destinations in the index, illustrating the complex trade-offs that potential relocators must consider.
