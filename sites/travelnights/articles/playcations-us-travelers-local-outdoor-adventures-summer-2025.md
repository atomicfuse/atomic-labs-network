## The Rise of Activity-Focused Getaways

A shift is happening in how Americans approach summer travel, with more travelers opting for what's being called "playcations"—short-haul trips centered around active outdoor adventures rather than traditional long-distance vacations. According to Airbnb's 2025 travel report, this trend is gaining momentum among US summer travelers who are choosing nearby destinations for hands-on activities like hiking, surfing, boating, and other outdoor pursuits.

## What's Driving the Playcation Trend

According to reports, this movement toward local, activity-based getaways reflects post-pandemic priorities around wellness, authenticity, and meaningful connection. Rather than spending days traveling to distant locations, Americans are discovering rich experiences closer to home that offer both physical activity and mental rejuvenation.

The trend also appeals to budget-conscious travelers seeking memorable experiences without the costs associated with long-haul travel. By staying closer to home, families and individuals can allocate more of their travel budget toward activities and experiences rather than transportation and lengthy accommodations.

## Popular Playcation Destinations

Several regions are emerging as playcation hotspots, according to the travel patterns identified in the report. Newport Beach offers coastal activities and water sports, while South Carolina provides diverse outdoor adventures from mountain hiking to coastal exploration. Colorado continues to attract active travelers with its renowned hiking and outdoor recreation opportunities, and Delaware is gaining recognition as an accessible destination for various outdoor activities.

These destinations share common characteristics: they're within driving distance for many Americans, offer diverse activity options, and provide the natural settings that playcation seekers crave.

## Health and Wellness Benefits

Expert insights highlighted in the research point to significant mental and social health benefits associated with outdoor play and active travel. According to reports, these activities help reduce stress while building genuine human connections—benefits that are particularly valued in our increasingly digital world.

The playcation model encourages unplugged time and shared outdoor challenges, which experts suggest can strengthen relationships and family bonds. Whether it's learning to paddle together, conquering a hiking trail, or trying surfing for the first time, these shared experiences create lasting memories and deeper connections.

## Activities Driving the Movement

Playcations encompass a wide range of outdoor activities, with paddling, hiking, surfing, and kayaking leading the way. These activities appeal to beginners and experienced enthusiasts alike, making playcations accessible to travelers with varying skill levels and fitness backgrounds.

The focus on hands-on activities distinguishes playcations from traditional passive tourism. Instead of simply sightseeing, travelers are engaging directly with their environment through physical challenges and skill-building experiences.

## Budget-Friendly Alternative

One of the most appealing aspects of the playcation trend is its financial accessibility. According to the insights gathered, staying closer to home delivers richer experiences while saving money typically spent on long-distance transportation and extended accommodations.

This economic advantage allows travelers to invest more in quality experiences, equipment rentals, guided activities, or premium local accommodations that enhance their active adventures.

## Looking Ahead

As summer travel planning reaches its peak, the playcation trend represents a fundamental shift in travel priorities. The combination of wellness benefits, budget considerations, and the desire for authentic experiences is driving more Americans to explore the outdoor adventure opportunities in their own regions.

This trend suggests that meaningful travel isn't necessarily about distance covered, but rather about the depth of experience and connection achieved. For travelers seeking active, engaging, and accessible summer adventures, playcations offer a compelling alternative to traditional vacation planning—proving that some of the best adventures might be closer than expected.
