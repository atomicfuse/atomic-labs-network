## A Hiking Guide That Doubles as Travel Planning

Outdoor travel continues to surge as a major wellness and lifestyle trend, with travelers seeking meaningful connections to nature close to home. Enter Stephanie Pearson's new National Geographic book, *100 Hikes of a Lifetime USA*, which according to reports blends hiking inspiration with practical travel-guide appeal for America's diverse trail systems.

The book arrives at an opportune moment as people increasingly plan national park visits, road trips, and active family vacations centered around outdoor experiences. Rather than simply listing trails, Pearson's guide takes a traveler-focused approach by highlighting standout routes for specific audiences across the United States.

## Trails Tailored to Your Travel Style

What sets this hiking guide apart is its focus on matching iconic U.S. trails to different traveler types. According to reports, the book features curated selections for families seeking accessible adventures, serious backpackers looking for multi-day challenges, van-lifers planning mobile basecamp trips, and RV travelers who need drive-up access to trailheads.

This approach recognizes that not every hiker has the same goals or capabilities. A family with young children needs different trail recommendations than hardcore adventurers seeking backcountry solitude, and the book reportedly addresses these varying needs throughout its pages.

## Beyond the Famous National Parks

While many hiking guides focus heavily on well-known national park destinations, reports suggest Pearson's collection includes underrated hikes that extend beyond the famous parks. This broader scope could prove valuable for travelers seeking to avoid crowds or discover hidden gems during their outdoor adventures.

The timing aligns with growing interest in dispersed outdoor recreation, as travelers look for alternatives to overcrowded popular destinations while still finding trails worthy of a "lifetime" designation.

For travelers planning hiking-focused road trips, the book reportedly serves dual purposes as both inspiration and practical planning tool. ## What Makes a Lifetime Hike

The "lifetime" designation suggests these aren't just any trails, but routes that offer something special worth the journey.
