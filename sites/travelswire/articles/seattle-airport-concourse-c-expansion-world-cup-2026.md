## Major Infrastructure Upgrade Debuts Ahead of Global Event

Seattle-Tacoma International Airport has unveiled its highly anticipated Concourse C expansion, a $399 million project that adds 145,000 square feet of new space just in time for the city's FIFA World Cup matches. The timing of this debut is strategic, as Seattle prepares to host World Cup games from June 15 to July 6, bringing an expected surge of international visitors to the Pacific Northwest.

According to reports, the new expansion focuses heavily on improving the traveler experience through diverse dining and retail options. The concourse incorporates local business kiosks, giving passengers a taste of Seattle's unique commercial landscape before they even leave the airport. This approach reflects the airport's commitment to showcasing regional character within its facilities.

One of the standout features of the expansion is an outdoor observation patio, providing travelers with a unique space to relax and take in views while waiting for their flights. This amenity addresses the growing demand for more varied and comfortable waiting areas in modern airports.
