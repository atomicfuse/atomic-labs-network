If you're a fan of engaging storytelling and powerful performances, Pedro Pascal's filmography is a treasure trove awaiting your discovery. This versatile actor's body of work spans various genres, showcasing his ability to immerse himself in diverse characters. From his breakout role in 'Game of Thrones' to his latest exploits in 'The Mandalorian,' Pedro Pascal has consistently delivered memorable performances. Here are five of his must-stream movies and TV shows that you absolutely cannot miss.

## 'Game of Thrones' – A Riveting Performance

Pedro Pascal's portrayal of Oberyn Martell, the charming yet lethal Prince of Dorne in 'Game of Thrones,' catapulted him to worldwide fame. His performance was marked by an infectious charisma and a thirst for vengeance, making his character unforgettable.

## 'Narcos' – A War Against Drugs

In 'Narcos,' Pedro Pascal plays the gritty DEA agent Javier Pena. His relentless pursuit of notorious drug lord Pablo Escobar is a high-stakes game that's as thrilling as it is dangerous. Pascal's performance brings authenticity and depth to this complex character.

## 'The Mandalorian' – A Lone Warrior in a Galaxy Far, Far Away

As the titular character in 'The Mandalorian,' Pedro Pascal ventures into the Star Wars universe. His portrayal of a bounty hunter navigating a chaotic galaxy while protecting a mysterious alien child has resonated with audiences worldwide.

## 'Prospect' – A Space Western

Another sci-fi gem featuring Pedro Pascal is 'Prospect.' This film sees him as a galactic drifter navigating dangerous terrain on a remote alien moon. His character's moral ambiguity adds an intriguing layer to this visually stunning space western.

## 'Triple Frontier' – A Tactical Heist Drama

In 'Triple Frontier,' Pedro Pascal joins forces with an ensemble cast to carry out a high-risk heist. This action-packed drama showcases Pascal's ability to excel in high-octane roles, further cementing his status as a versatile performer.

---

Pedro Pascal's rich and diverse filmography offers something for everyone. Whether you're a fan of fantasy, crime dramas, or sci-fi, there's a Pedro Pascal movie or TV show just waiting to be streamed. So grab your remote, get comfortable, and prepare for an unforgettable viewing experience.
