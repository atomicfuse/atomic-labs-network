## Market Positioning and Competition

Reports indicate the 2028 Xterra will target the hot off-road SUV segment, where vehicles like the Jeep Wrangler and Ford Bronco have seen strong sales success. The Toyota 4Runner has also maintained a loyal following in this space, creating an established competitive landscape that Nissan will need to navigate.

The timing appears strategic, as off-road capable SUVs continue to attract buyers seeking adventure-ready vehicles that can handle both daily driving and weekend outdoor activities.
