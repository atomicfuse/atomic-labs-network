## Performance Issues with LED Steering Wheel Cover

The testing revealed several significant problems with the LED steering wheel cover. According to the creator's review, the accessory suffered from slow startup times, making it less convenient for immediate use. Additionally, the charging process was described as awkward, potentially creating hassles for daily drivers.

Installation proved to be another major hurdle. Reports indicate that the creator encountered notable difficulties when attempting to properly install the LED cover on their steering wheel, raising questions about the product's user-friendliness and practical design.

## Rising Safety Concerns Around Steering Wheel Modifications

The review has sparked broader discussions about safety concerns surrounding steering wheel covers and LED accessories in vehicles. According to reports, automotive safety experts have raised questions about the potential risks of adding aftermarket accessories to critical driving components like steering wheels.

Steering wheel modifications can potentially interfere with airbag deployment, affect grip during emergency maneuvers, or create distractions for drivers. The LED lights, while aesthetically appealing, may also contribute to visual distractions during nighttime driving.
