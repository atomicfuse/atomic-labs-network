Looking for the perfect roasted vegetables recipe? This easy sheet pan dinner pairs beautifully roasted vegetables with a creamy tahini yogurt dressing for a flavor-packed and healthy meal. Perfect for meal prep, weeknight dinners, or as a holiday side dish, this recipe is versatile, nutritious, and incredibly delicious.

## Why You'll Love This Recipe

- Simple to prepare and loaded with flavor.
- Perfectly roasted vegetables every time!
- Pairs with a creamy tahini yogurt dressing for an irresistible twist.

## Ingredients You'll Need

- Mixed vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, carrots, etc.)
- Olive oil
- Salt, pepper, and your favorite spices
- Tahini yogurt dressing ingredients (tahini, yogurt, garlic, lemon juice)

## Pro Tips for Success

- Spread vegetables in a single layer for even roasting.
- Roast at high heat for perfectly caramelized veggies.
- Adjust the seasoning to your taste for the dressing.
