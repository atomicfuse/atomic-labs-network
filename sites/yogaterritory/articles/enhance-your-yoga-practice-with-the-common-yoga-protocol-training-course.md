The Common Yoga Protocol Training Course is a unique opportunity for yoga enthusiasts to deepen their practice and understanding of yoga. Held on the occasion of the International Day of Yoga 2025, this course is organized by Shree H.V.P. Mandal's Degree College of Physical Education in Amravati, Maharashtra, India. As a multi-faculty autonomous college, Shree H.V.P. Mandal's Degree College offers a comprehensive program that is both educational and enriching for participants.

This training course is designed to align with the International Day of Yoga, which is celebrated globally to promote the physical, mental, and spiritual benefits of practicing yoga. Participants in this course will learn the Common Yoga Protocol, a standardized set of yoga practices designed to be accessible to people of all ages and skill levels. The protocol emphasizes holistic wellness and the integration of mind, body, and spirit.

## What to Expect from the Training

The Common Yoga Protocol Training Course includes a series of sessions that cover various aspects of yoga, from basic postures to advanced techniques. Participants will receive guidance from experienced instructors who will provide insights into the philosophy of yoga and its practical applications. This course is a great opportunity for both beginners and seasoned practitioners to enhance their skills and knowledge.

## Benefits of Participating

Engaging in the Common Yoga Protocol Training Course offers numerous benefits. Not only does it improve physical fitness and flexibility, but it also promotes mental clarity and emotional stability. The practices taught in this course can lead to a more balanced lifestyle, reduce stress, and improve overall well-being.

## Join the Global Yoga Community

By participating in this training course, you become part of a larger global community that values health, wellness, and personal growth. International Day of Yoga serves as a reminder of the universal appeal of yoga and its ability to bring people together across cultures and borders.

Whether you are new to yoga or looking to deepen your practice, the Common Yoga Protocol Training Course is an ideal way to celebrate International Day of Yoga and invest in your personal development.
